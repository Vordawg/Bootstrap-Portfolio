About Me

I am a developer, technical leader, and business analyst at The Hartford in Stamford, Connecticut. As a developer my responsibilities included: incorporating agreed changes into requirement specification and inform the affected team members; manage releases together with the project manager, ensure that change requests and bug fixes are weighed up against time and budget restrictions of the project; and ensure complete, up to date technical product documentation. I work in close collaboration with the business users to fully understand the business requirements that have to be reflected in the design and implementation; and review the business requirements continuously and identify unclear, incomplete, redundant, unrealistic or inconsistent requirements. As a technical leader, I support the users during user acceptance test and prioritize defects during user acceptance test; training new developers; developing and enhancing programming guidelines; conduct code reviews with all new hires and consultants to ensure version control of source code; and ensure the quality of deliverables.

I currently have over 20 years experience as an Application Developer. I have an extensive amount of experience in coding, debugging, creating test plans and assisting with regressing testing and production support assisting the business users. I am quick to learn and eager to put my knowledge to work. Some of my business characteristics include commitment to self-improvement in order to achieve the highest levels of personal excellence, quality and integrity of business requirements and deliverables, the use of interpersonal skills to promote teamwork and business user satisfaction, and a strong sense of ownership and accountability for a job well done.
